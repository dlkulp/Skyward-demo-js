Sneaky Snake:
A procedurally generated game first written for the CodeDay:Portland in February of 2015 at Prolifiq using the custom-made Argyle Engine. The game is by no means dead and I still work on it in my spare time!

Objective:
Sneak around the little guys to make your way to the teleporter to bring you to the next level. If you don't see a teleporter make sure to check all the corners! Walking over the little ones increases your score and speed but also makes you longer. Plan carefully!

Controls:
w, s, a, d to move around, if the controls are confusing try clicking the Toggle Controls button to switch them.
m to mute
r to restart
t to toggle controls
